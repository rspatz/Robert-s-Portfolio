Celiac Disease Helpline Website

Contact Me

Today, the popular press gives the impression that people who are diagnosed with Celiac Disease have more access to gluten-free food and information than at any time in history, and perhaps that's true. However, the gluten-free lifestyle still has many pitfalls, and many are being misinformed and misled, particularly by websites, books, and articles claiming that the gluten-free diet is easy to manaage. Thus, there is a definite need for a website that attempts to assist and educate the newly-diagnosed, while offering support, and enabling them to support each other.
